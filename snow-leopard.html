This is the last Version of pearOS, developerd by David Tavares, in year 2016.. The design was "inspired" by Apple Inc. The previous version (pearOS 8) was made by David Tavares.in 2014. Back to 2019, I decided to revive this project, because it's so cool, in my opinion. The base os for this is KDE Neon 18.04. This distro have all Ubuntu and Kde repositories, so, you won't miss any Security, Driver or Application update. Keep in mind that this is a beta version, so bugs may ocour. Thanks for trying pearOS 10.15.4 Catalina. Stay tuned for pearOS Cupertino (Anniversary Edition, august 2020)
